# Products

Nazaraa offers a comprehensive suite of visualization and material selection tools designed for homeowners, interior designers, and retailers.

## Our Platform

Our digital visualization platform enables users to visualize materials in real spaces, compare options, and make informed decisions about their interior design projects.

## Product Categories

### Flooring Visualization

Upload your room photos and see how different floor tiles and materials look in your actual space with photorealistic accuracy. Our AI-powered technology ensures realistic rendering that helps you make confident choices.

### Wall Tile Visualization

Visualize wall tiles and materials on your walls before making a purchase. See how different textures, colors, and patterns transform your space.

### Material Library

Access a comprehensive library of materials from leading manufacturers. Browse thousands of options for floors, walls, and other surfaces.

### Cost Estimation

Get instant cost estimates for your selected materials based on room measurements. Calculate material requirements and total costs to help with budgeting and planning.

## Features

- **Photorealistic Rendering**: Advanced AI technology for accurate material visualization
- **Side-by-Side Comparison**: Compare multiple material options easily
- **Instant Cost Estimates**: Calculate material requirements and costs
- **Comprehensive Material Library**: Access to thousands of materials
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Intuitive design for all skill levels
