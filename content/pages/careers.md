# Careers at Nazaraa

Join us in revolutionizing the interior design and material visualization industry. We're building the future of how people select and visualize materials for their spaces.

## Why Work at Nazaraa?

### Innovation at the Core

Work on cutting-edge AI and visualization technology that's transforming an entire industry. You'll be part of a team that's pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

### Impact

Your work directly helps homeowners, designers, and retailers make better decisions. See your contributions make a real difference in people's lives and businesses.

### Growth Opportunities

We're a fast-growing company with opportunities for professional development, learning, and career advancement.

### Collaborative Culture

Join a team of passionate, talented individuals who support each other and work together to achieve common goals.

## Join Our Team

Join Nazaraa and help shape the future of design visualization!

Interested in joining our team? Send your resume and a brief intro to **support@nazaraa.in**

### What We Look For

- Passion for technology and innovation
- Strong problem-solving skills
- Collaborative mindset
- Eagerness to learn and grow
- Alignment with our values

## Our Hiring Process

1. **Application Review**: We review all applications carefully and respond to candidates who match our requirements.

2. **Initial Interview**: A brief conversation to learn more about you and share more about the role.

3. **Technical Assessment**: For technical roles, we may ask you to complete a relevant assessment.

4. **Team Interview**: Meet with team members to discuss the role and see if there's a good fit.

5. **Final Decision**: We make our decision and extend an offer to successful candidates.

- Opportunity to work with cutting-edge technology
