# Cookie Policy

Nazaraa uses cookies to enhance your experience.

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your device to help our website function properly, remember your preferences, and gather analytics.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

- Essential cookies ensure the website is usable
- Performance cookies help us analyze traffic and usage
- Preference cookies remember settings for your convenience

## 3. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies using your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

## 4. Cookie Consent

By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies as described. You may manage consent or learn more via our privacy policy.
